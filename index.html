Louisiana State University in Shreveport (LSU Shreveport or LSUS) is a branch institution of the Louisiana State University System located in Shreveport, Louisiana. With an enrollment of 4,082 students, LSUS is a multipurpose system of higher education, accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools.
